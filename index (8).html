The Firm

Australia's first.
Still making waves.

Genesis Block Advisory was founded in June 2021 — the moment Australia's virtual asset industry needed a dedicated regulatory affairs practice and none existed. We were first, and we built the template others followed.

We serve exchanges, brokerages, asset managers, listed companies, and early-stage start-ups navigating Australia's digital asset regulatory landscape. Our work spans policy submissions, government relations, regulatory compliance, risk management, and executive briefings across the full digital asset value chain.

Our most significant contribution to Australian policy: the most-cited independent submission to the Senate's Bragg Inquiry — 46 citations, more than any other industry paper — directly resulting in adopted recommendations that paved the path for Australia's Digital Assets Framework Bill.

Founder

Chloe White

Chloe White is one of Australia's most credentialled experts at the intersection of government policy, financial markets, and emerging technology — the economist who helped build digital asset regulation from the ground up, in Australia and internationally.

Over a 15-year career spanning Commonwealth Treasury, the Australian Government's National Blockchain Roadmap, and a foundational advisory role at Dubai's Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA), she has shaped the rules that govern the digital asset industry across multiple jurisdictions. Following the success of Genesis Block's launch in Australia, Chloe founded her global practice, Riskmastery, and now serves clients internationally.

She is recognised in the Top 50 Most Influential Australians in Blockchain three consecutive years (2023, 2024, 2025), is a dual Blockies Award winner, and is represented by the Saxton Group — Australia's premier speakers' bureau.
